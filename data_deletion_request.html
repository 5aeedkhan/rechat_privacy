How to Delete Your Data

ReChat stores all your data locally on your device. You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time using the methods below.

‚úÖ Complete Control: All your data is stored locally on your device. No data is stored on our servers or in the cloud.
Method 1: Delete Data from Within the App
1 Open ReChat app
2 Go to Settings (tap the settings icon in the app)
3 Navigate to Developer Tools
4 Select "Complete Data Wipe" to delete all data for all monitored apps
5 Confirm deletion when prompted
Method 2: Delete Data from Android Settings
1 Open Android Settings
2 Go to Apps & notifications (or "Apps" on some devices)
3 Find and tap "ReChat"
4 Tap "Storage"
5 Tap "Clear Storage" or "Clear Data"
6 Confirm deletion when prompted
Method 3: Uninstall the App

The simplest way to delete all your data is to uninstall the ReChat app:

1 Long press the ReChat app icon on your home screen
2 Select "Uninstall" or drag to the uninstall area
3 Confirm uninstallation
Note: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all your recovered messages, media, and app settings.
What Data Gets Deleted

When you delete your data, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • All recovered messages from WhatsApp, Telegram, Messenger, Instagram, and other supported apps
  • All deleted message records and recovery history
  • All media files (images, videos, audio, documents) saved by the app
  • All WhatsApp statuses saved by the app
  • All app settings and configuration data
  • All monitoring preferences and selected apps
  • All keyword tracking data and search history
  • All debug logs and diagnostic information

Data Retention Policy

ReChat follows a strict local-only data policy:

  • No cloud storage: We do not store any of your data on our servers
  • No data transmission: Your data never leaves your device
  • Immediate deletion: When you delete data, it is removed immediately from your device
  • No retention period: We do not keep any copies of your data
  • Complete user control: You decide when and how to delete your data
