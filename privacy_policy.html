1. Overview

ReChat is built with privacy by design. We process your notifications locally on your device so you can view, recover, and organize messages from supported apps when messages are changed or deleted.

2. Data We Collect (On Your Device)
  • Notifications content and metadata sent to your device by Android from supported apps (e.g., WhatsApp, WhatsApp Business, Telegram, Messenger, Instagram).
  • Sender information and conversation titles derived from notifications. For WhatsApp groups, we may infer group names using recent context when notifications omit them.
  • Media presence and file paths when you grant media/storage permissions needed to scan known app folders for recovery.
  • We do not access your device contacts. Names are derived from notifications when available.
3. How We Process Data
  • All processing happens on-device. We parse notifications to store messages and statuses locally (e.g., in a local database).
  • We implement deletion handling including app-initiated deletion detection and tap-open suppression to avoid false deletion records.
  • We maintain short-lived, in-memory context (e.g., recent group mapping ~90s) to improve classification when notifications are incomplete.
4. No Cloud Upload or Sale

Your data stays on your device. We do not upload your message content to our servers, and we do not sell or rent your data. Any export/logs you choose to share are initiated by you and leave the device under your control.
